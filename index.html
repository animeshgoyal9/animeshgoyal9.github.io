About me

I'm a Data Scientist / Machine Learning enthusiast based in Palo Alto California, USA.

My field of interests is Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning. I have been working on multiple projects related to classification and prediction models. Working as a Data Researcher at USC IMSC Labs I analyzed human behavior during disaster situation.
I interned at Archie.AI an Artificial Intelligence start-up, in San Francisco. Here I built Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN) and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to make Archie.AI better in analysing business data.
While working on these projects, I published a technical blog on one of the neural networks I was working on. It was published on Medium.com and was featured under Artificial Intelligence section with more than 15k views and upvotes.
Further at Archie.ai, I released three tutorial videos for anyone interested to understand the concepts of Machine Learning and build their own models within few minutes. All three of them combined have got more than 25K views and I do plan to release more such tutorial videos and blogs.
So if you have a position related to Machine Learning, Deep Learning or Software Development open for which I could be the right fit, I would love to discuss it.
