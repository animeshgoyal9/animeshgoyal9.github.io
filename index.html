About me

I'm a Data Scientist / Machine Learning enthusiast based in Austin, TX, USA.

My field of interest is Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Deep Learning. For the past few years, I have been working on multiple data-driven projects related to classification and prediction models. Currently, as a Graduate Research Assistant at UTCS Artificial Intelligence Lab, I am working on improving the decision making process in a complex, dynamic and multi-agent environment such as a disaster space using Reinforcement learning. The Project involves integration of additional functionalities to several thousand lines of code in Robocup Rescue simulator
Along with my research work, I have been participating in various Hackathons and Kaggle competitions. We, as a team of 4, won the Data Hack Challenge 2019 organized by Microsoft Azure, Oracle and ML-DS group at UT Austin.
While working on these projects, I published my first technical blog on one of the Recommendation engine projects I was working on. The blog was published on Medium.com and it got featured by the Medium team under Towards Data Science section. The article became a hit with more than 10k views and upvotes.
I am looking for full time opportunities starting May 2020. So, if you have a position related to Machine Learning, Deep Learning or Artificial Intelligence open for which I could be the right fit, I would love to discuss it.
